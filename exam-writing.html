My city is Baghdad. It is located in the centre of Iraq. Baghdad is a very old and famous city. It is known for the Al-Mutanabbi Street where you can find many books and poets. My city has many museums and beautiful mosques. I love Baghdad because it has a long history and wonderful people. I hope my city will be more beautiful in the future.
